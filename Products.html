Our Products and services

Zahara Africa is anchored on the growing need of Self-publishing, Early Childhood Materials, CBC, special needs education and TIVET content.

1. Special needs education learning materials

We have partnered with development partners to publish books on special needs education for specific learning areas. Experienced experts are involved in the authorship and content creation.

2. Early Childhood Development & Education

Customised ECD materials have been developed to take care of different and unique learning needs in identified regions. They include course books, activity books, and charts and play material. Unique cultural, environmental, geographical and social diversities are included in content development.


3. CBC junior and senior secondary(Grade 7 to 12)

Competence based curriculum books developed in line with the ministry of education curriculum guidelines in Kenya and Botswana.


4. Reference for ECD, primary and secondary

Reference products that include dictionaries, atlases, and general books have been availed to our customers through license printing and distribution rights by our partners.

5. Tertiary and TIVET learning materials

TIVET books have been developed for different regions and countries in line with specific curriculum guidelines. Experienced instructors are involved in authorship.


6. Self-publishing( freelance authors)

The need for individual publishing has necessitated formation of a publishing service division. Expert editorial and design services are offered to clients who wish to publish their content, mostly Biographies, Autobiographies memoirs and many more.


Additional Information

1.We have vast experience in ECDE, Primary, secondary and tertiary education segments in terms of content creation, markets and distribution channels.

2. To achieve the envisaged goals, partnerships with various organisations, governments, and individuals are being explored and executed.

3. Customised ECD, TIVET and CBC learning materials are developed and distributed as per the guidelines given by governments, NGOs, churches, individual donors to collectively achieve curriculum requirements.

4. Education and curriculum experts are involved in the content development process.

5. We provide distribution services to already published and approved books through rights and licence printing arrangements.
